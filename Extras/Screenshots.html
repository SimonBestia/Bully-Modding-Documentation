All Screenshots

Archival project originally started on SWEGTA's BullyMods forum, then moved to Bully-Board, and finally, here.
Without having to worry about other sites going down, here's hoping this one lasts forever!

Most of the screenshots are hosted on Imgur, in case you want to download them.
Note that some are not included and are grabbed from elsewhere, like IGN's website.

Only digitally-released screenshots will be included.
Including ones available only in magazines is impractical as various scans differ in colour, quality, size...

This will take a long time and will probably never be completed due to the sheer number of screenshots Rockstar Games released.
Any help on finding more would be greatly appreciated!

Long-term Goals:

  • There are (probably) WAY more to find, still.
  • Replace every watermarked screenshot with its original, watermark-less and uncompressed version.
